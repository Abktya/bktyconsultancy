1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on users' devices. These files are used for the website to function properly and provide better service to you.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your login status
  • Security Cookies: Prevent CSRF attacks
  • Language Preference: Remember selected language
  • Cookie Consent: Store your cookie preferences

These cookies cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand site performance:

  • Page view statistics
  • User behavior analysis
  • Error reporting
  • Site speed measurement

You can opt out of these cookies.

Marketing Cookies

For providing personalized content:

  • Interest-based recommendations
  • Email marketing personalization
  • Social media integrations

You can opt out of these cookies.

Functional Cookies

For enhanced features:

  • User preference settings
  • AI assistant chat history
  • Form auto-fill
  • Theme and appearance settings

You can opt out of these cookies.
