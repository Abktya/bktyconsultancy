Privacy Policy

How SwipeFoto Protects Your Privacy and Data

🔒 100% Local Processing 🛡️ GDPR Compliant 📱 No Cloud Upload

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Introduction

BKTY LTD ("we", "our", or "us") operates SwipeFoto ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Privacy First

SwipeFoto is designed with privacy in mind. Your photos never leave your device, and we collect minimal information only to provide essential app functionality.

Information We Collect

SwipeFoto collects minimal information to function properly:

App Preferences
  • ✓ Your settings and folder configurations
  • ✓ Stored locally on your device only
  • ✓ Never transmitted to external servers
Photo Library Access
  • ✓ Access granted only with your permission
  • ✓ All photos remain on your device
  • ✓ Photos processed entirely locally
Usage Analytics
  • ✓ Anonymous usage data via AdMob
  • ✓ Used to improve app performance
  • ✓ No personally identifiable information
Device Information
  • ✓ Basic device type and iOS version
  • ✓ For compatibility purposes only
  • ✓ No unique identifiers stored

Photo Library Access

100% Local Processing

Your photos are processed entirely on your device. We never access, view, upload, or store your photos on external servers.

SwipeFoto requires access to your photo library to organize your photos. This access is:

  • Requested only when necessary
  • Limited to what you explicitly grant
  • Used solely for photo organization features
  • Revocable at any time through iOS Settings

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud Photo Library enabled on your device, your photos and albums are automatically synced by Apple's iCloud service.

Important

SwipeFoto does not control or manage iCloud sync. This is handled entirely by iOS. We do not have access to your iCloud account or photos stored in iCloud.

Advertising & Analytics

SwipeFoto uses Google AdMob to display advertisements and collect analytics data. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage statistics
Premium Users

Premium subscribers receive an ad-free experience with no AdMob tracking.

For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy

Data Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures:

Local-only data storage
No external data transmission
iOS security framework protection
Regular security updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access your data stored in the App

Delete your data by deleting the App

Opt-out of analytics in device settings

Request information about collected data

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the App removes all associated data permanently.

Children's Privacy

SwipeFoto does not knowingly collect information from children. The App is suitable for all ages as it:

  • Does not generate or share content
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Processes all data locally
  • Requires parental supervision for children
